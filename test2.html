    THE INTRO

    Hi! I'm Ediubong Ekwere and my art name is Juno and Ice.
    Born in Houston, Texas, I am a 24 year-old freelance artist, full-stack web developer, and game developer.

    Since I was young, I was always fascinated with computers, and their ability to create almost anything within our wildest imagination.

    Throughout my life, I was able to consume all types of media, from video games, to cartoons and anime, to live-action film. All made possible by the power of computer code, and great minds coming together to create a something new.

    I also want to bring my crazy imaginations to life.
