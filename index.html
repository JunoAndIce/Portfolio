    THE INTRO

    Hi! I'm Ediubong Ekwere and my art name is Juno and Ice.
    Born in Houston, Texas, I am a 24 year-old freelance artist, full-stack web developer, and game developer.

    Since I was young, I was always fascinated with computers, and their ability to create almost anything within our wildest imagination.

    THE PHILOSOPHY

    There are 3 key philosophies I always keep in mind when working on a project of any caliber.

  • Never forget the small details. While the overall goal is always important, the small details give every project it's own identity.
  • Stay open-minded. Always be open to changing plans if things don't work out, and always be willing to hear other point of views.
  • Put 200% into every project. Whether or not the project is my own, or a commission for someone else, there is never a reason to not go all out on said project. I will always put my all into every project I undertake.

    Chronoscape

    Project Description:

    Chronoscape is a Handlebars.js and Express.js choose your own adventure game built with Node.js, Express.js, MySQL, and Sequelize. The game features a short but interesting story and has the user travel through very familiar locations.

    Chronoscape was the second project of my UTA Coding Bootcamp and challenged me and my fellow classmates to understand how to use handlebars.js and express.js to build a web application. We chose to build a choose your own adventure game because past students had failed to complete the project, and we took it upon ourselves to truly understand handlebars and implement it like never before.
